Integrity and Anti-Corruption Course

This course covers the basics of corruption, including integrity, anti-corruption, types of corrupt practices, abuse of power in daily activities and organizations, as well as methods of prevention. Real-life corruption cases are also discussed. Teaching is conducted through experiential learning with individual and group activities. By the end of the course, students will understand the principles of integrity, the concept of corruption, and strategies for preventing abuse of power in society and organizations.
