Discrete Structure

This course introduces the fundamentals of discrete structures in computer science, covering topics like set theory, proof techniques, relations, functions, recurrence relations, counting methods, graph theory, trees, and finite automata. Students will learn to apply these concepts to solve problems, model systems, and address real-world challenges in computer science.
